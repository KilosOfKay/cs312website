Introduction

Why This Project:

This website will explore my paired reading selections through the lense of my Human Design (HD) chart. Paired reading is a new hobby I've taken up after coming across a TikToker whose thesis for her Masters program was on metaphors and caused her to also dive into paired reading as a topic of exploration and teaching. And I've recently learned about HD and have been exploring parts of my chart to test out in my life. And finally, I think this is the second best time to be a content creator or some sort. So, I want to test concepts I've learned about creating content for social media using the thoughts and creations from my book pairings and HD as a part of my strategy.

More On My Human Design:

In Human design I have shores environment, power perspective, and the storyteller channel. The shore environment is a symbol of two things meeting, like two (or more) books. It's also about existing in/near two places at once, like when you read. My power perspective is about seeing who is winning, and how. However, it is nutured through the flexing of my creative skills, reviewing and sharing my favorite things, and sharing (or gossiping) about people (or characters) whose lives interest me.

The Social Media Part:

For exploration of creating content I'll mostly focus on Instagram and TikTok. Sometimes I may engage with authors and groups on Twitter (I'm not calling it "X") and Pages. The themes and topics from the books I read tend to give me a lot to respond to in action or thought. Therefore, I will not limit this blog to just blog posts. It will feature exploration of music, podcasts and other media that I feel relates, I want to share, or react to.
